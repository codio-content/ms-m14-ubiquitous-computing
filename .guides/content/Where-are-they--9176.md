## what data & where
 - Medical information - in hospital wrist bands, etc
  - Race timing - RFID on runners
   - Access control - RFID in access cards
 - Kisoks for DVD rentals
  - package tracking for mailings and load shipments
  - Real time location  - tracking animals in wild or even employees at work
  
These are just a small sample of where RFID chips are being used. There is no limit to where they can be used and what information they may hold.
